## Physical self-care?!

Taking care of your own physical needs

Home Physical Mental Games
Taking care of your body plays an important part
in improving your mental wellness,
as it helps you in managing stress, lowers your chances of
getting ill and helps to increase your energy levels!

Sports and physical
activities

an activity involving physical exertion and skill as an individual or a team

Doing sports and physical helps in improving your mood,
sleep schedule and mental focus as well.
All of this helps you lead a better and more balanced lifestyle!
Here are some simple sports and physical activities you
can try doing during your free time!

Jogging/ Running

Swimming

Indoor/Outdoor cycling

Did you know that simple physical activities
such as going for a walk and doing household
chores are also a form of physical activity?

Like what they say, "Any exercise is better than none".

Eating well

Eating a wide variety of foods in the right proportions,
and consuming the right amount of food and drink to achieve
and maintain a healthy body weight

Having a balanced diet can make you feel and think better,
it improves our mood and increases our attention span.
More importantly, we are less likely to feel tired and distracted easily.

How to have a healthy diet!

Eating regularly

Avoid skipping meals as it would make you feel tired
and possibly lead to you being in a bad mood

Eating your fruits,
veggies and protein

Try to include all of them in your meals
in order to have a fullfilling one

Staying hydrated

Drink at least 2 litres of water
(or more!) on a daily basis

Getting enough sleep

Getting good quality sleep for an adequate amount of hours

Lack of sleep is one of the core factors that affect mental wellness.
When one lacks enough sleep, they may have trouble making decisions,
focusing, and have difficulty in controlling their emotions as well.

If you have difficulty falling asleep,
don’t worry, we got your back :)
Here are some tips on how to get better sleep

Try to have a more
stable sleep routine

Sleeping and waking
up at around the
same time everyday

Avoid using your electronic
devices before sleeping

Meditate before
