What is mental wellness?

Mental wellness is ensuring that our mind is in order and is functioning at our best interest.
It plays a very big part on our overall health as well, and should not be overlooked.

Mental health VS Mental wellness

Mental health is the overall state of
our mental functioning.
Mental wellness means achieving a
positive state of mind in a holistic sense.

Importance of having
mental wellness

    It is crucial to have a positive mindset as
    mental wellness affects how we think, feel and act.
    Mental wellness also plays a part in our overall health and helps
    us find purpose in life, making sense of the things that we do

Common factors that can affect
your mental wellness

Lack of sleep


When individuals lack sufficient sleep, they would easily get irritated, have frequent mood swings and have difficulty thinking and concentrating. They may also feel more sensitive and are prone to feeling sad and angry easily.

Feeling stressed

Stressed individuals would have an increased exposure to medical problems such as headaches and weak immune systems. They are also at risk of suffering from mental health problems such as anxiety and depression, and may start to rely on substances to 'destress'.

Loneliness

Individuals can feel lonely even if they are surrounded by their family and friends. This is because they may feel that they are not seen, unheard or misunderstood by their peers. This then results in individuals having a higher chance of experiencing depression and anxiety.
